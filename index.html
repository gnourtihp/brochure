Présentation du voyage

Le mot de l'expert :

Ce circuit vous emmène au cœur du Vietnam, pays des mille contrastes et des paysages à couper le souffle. De Hanoi et son quartier ancien chargé d'histoire à la majesté de la Baie d'Halong, en passant par les ruelles dorées de Hoi An, les vestiges impériaux de Hué et l'effervescence de Ho Chi Minh-Ville, c'est un voyage entre immersion culturelle, découvertes historiques et rencontres humaines qui marquent durablement. Un itinéraire qui va bien au-delà, pour vivre le Vietnam dans toute son authenticité.

Itinéraire

DAY 01: SAIGON ARRIVAL (D)

Arrive at Tan Son Nhat International Airport; you will be welcomed by our guide and transfer to the city center. Check-in hotel and then relax or have your first-hand discovery of the bustling and hustling city.

Dinner at a local restaurant. 

Overnight at hotel in Saigon.

 

DAY 02: SAIGON – CU CHI TUNNELS – CITY TOUR (B.L.D)

After breakfast at hotel, head out to Cu Chi Tunnels which are an immense network of connecting underground tunnels located about 60km from Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon), and are part of a much larger network of tunnels that underlie much of the country. The Cu Chi tunnels were the location of several military campaigns during the Vietnam War. It is legendary for its tunnels system of over 200km. You will have an opportunity to crawl in hundreds meters of the tunnels system in some levels with innumerable trap doors, storage facilities, weapon factories, field hospitals, kitchens and command centers. Also, you can participate in fire off rounds from an AK47 or MK16 at the nearby firing range (at your own cost).

Then, transfer back to Saigon, start city tour by walking along Dong Khoi St, which used to be known as the Rue Catinat, the first street built by Frech. Next visit European style architectures including Notre Dame Cathedral and the Central Post Office.

Later, dip into the modern history at Reunification Palace which is also called Independence Palace or Doc Lap Palace in Vietnamese. The Palace was stormed by tanks of the liberation army on April 30 1975. Nowadays, Reunification Palace has been a historic and cultural relic. Continue the trip to War Remnants Museum which preserves the items and documents in the Vietnam – American War.

 

DAY 03: SAIGON – VINH TRANG TEMPLE – MY THO – TRA VINH (B.L.D)

Our guide and driver pick you up at your hotel; head out to My Tho in Mekong Delta. Dip into the beauty of rice fields along the way. Firstly, visit Vinh Trang pagoda which is the “must visit” site in Tien Giang. The pagoda is an ancient Southern gem with the combination of Asian and European architecture including Renaissance period and Romanesque style.

Next, continue to My Tho, up on arrival, take a boat trip on Mekong River to visit farmer’s house and garden. View the fishing port, the traditional stilt houses and boat building workshops and islands including Dragon, Phoenix, Unicorn and Tortoise.

Enjoy lunch on the beautiful island.

Next, ride horse and carriage around villages to see the local life; taste tropical fruits and honey tea at bee-keeping farm; listen to traditional music and visit coconut candy manufacturer. Roam on village path with fruit orchards and bonsai garden. Meet the locals to get deeper understanding about their daily life in the watery area.

Continue your trip to Tra Vinh Lodge where you will immerse in the peace and beauty of the countryside. Enjoy the beauty of countryside during your travel by boat to Tra Vinh Lodge. After check-in the bungalow, have a short bike ride or wander around the area surrounded by orchards, rice fields, canals and lines of coconut tree. After that have a cooking demonstration in which you can learn to make Vietnamese food.

Diner and overnight at Tra Vinh Lodge. 

 

DAY 04: TRA VINH – CAN THO – CON SON ISLAND - HOMESTAY (B/-/D)

After your breakfast, head out to the center of Tra Vinh. Visit Ao Ba Om (Ba Om Lake), Khmer Culture Museum, Co (Stork) and Hang (cave) temples. Continue traveling to Can Tho to visit Ong Pagoda which is one of the most important religious sites of Chinese community in Can Tho. It is built in the late 19th century to worship Kuang Kung, a deity symbolizing loyalty, justice, reason, intelligence, honor and courage among other merits. Then, visit Binh Thuy ancient house. Binh Thuy ancient house was built in 1870 with French style in architecture but still remained traditional features of Southern style. This is the private property of Duong’s family, it however has been acknowledged as a valuable construction of the city and nation for its architectural and historical values.

Later, take the boat to Con Son island where you have overnight at homestay and stay away from the bustling and noisy city. Here, you will see the real authenticity with farming activities. There are only under 100 households on the isalnd. There is no vehicle here and people have to walk from this place to others. Before dinner, you can join cooking with the host to learn Vietnamese dishes. Overnight at homestay in Can Tho.
